How We Work

Our Smooth Design Process

From initial site measurements to final key handover, we follow a transparent step-by-step approach.

01
Phase One

Initial Consultation

We discuss your space ideas, functional requirements, color preferences, and layout budget during an in-person meeting.

02
Phase Two

Site Measurements & Planning

Our design experts visit your property in Khargone to record precise laser measurements and map doors, windows, and electrical points.

03
Phase Three

Design Concept & 2D Layouts

We develop architectural plan options and select color styles. We align materials with you to ensure it fits your style.

04
Phase Four

Material & Finish Selection

You choose exact laminates (high-gloss mica, wood textures, acrylic), wallpapers, false ceiling profiles, and handle accessories from our catalog.

05
Phase Five

Crafting & On-Site Execution

Our carpentry and paint teams begin structural work, installing premium panels, false ceilings, electrical loops, and wallpapers under site supervision.

06
Phase Six

Final Quality Check & Delivery

After thorough cleaning, drawer alignment checks, and lighting tests, we hand over the gorgeous, royal space to you.
